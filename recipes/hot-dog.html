Hot Dog Recipe

Description

A North American classic, this outdoor favorite will get all the people (and pets)mouth's watering.

Not intended for children under 3

Ingredients

  • 1 Hot Dog Bun
  • 1 All-Beef Hot Dog
  • 1 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1 tbsp Mustard
  • 50g cabbage....apparently

Steps

  1. On your preferred heat source, cook the all beef hot dog on medium-high heat for 5-10 minutes, until the outside is slightly crispy but not burnt.
  2. While the hot dog is cooking, open up your hot dog bun and add the cabbage.
  3. Place hotdog on top of cabbage in hot dog bun.
  4. gently drizzle ketchup and mustard on top of hot dog. Serve warm.
