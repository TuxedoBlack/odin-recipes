Hot Dog Recipe

Description

An American Classic, the hot dog belongs at every BBQ, Ball Game and greasy picnic alike!

Ingredients

    • White Bun
      Beef or Pork Weiner
      ketchup
      Mustard

    • Directions

    • Heat BBQ to 450 degrees F
      place weiner on low grill and bun on high grill
      cook for 5-10 minutes, or until the weiner is crisp and crackling
      place 'hot dog' on bun, cover in mustard and ketchup
