Vegan Trifle Recipe

Description

Treat guests to this plant-based trifle, with layers of sponge, compote, custard and dairy-free cream. It's a great dessert for a celebration!

Dairy-free, Egg-free, Vegan, Vegetarian

Ingredients

For the sponge cake:

  • 50ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 200ml plant milk
  • 125g caster sugar
  • 200g self-raising flour

For the compote:

  • 400g raspberries
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

For the custard:

  • 50g cornflour
  • 500ml plant milk
  • 75g caster sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
  • a pinch turmeric

For the cream:

  • 250ml whippable plant cream
  • 2-3 tsp icing sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla bean paste
